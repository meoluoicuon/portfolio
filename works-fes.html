Le Hong Phong Music Festival 2018

Le Hong Phong Music Festival is an annual event organized mainly by alumni and current students at Le Hong Phong high school for the gifted, HCMC, Vietnam. Being hold the first time in 2008, LHP Festival is now one of the largest music festivals exclusively for students in HCMC with high sound and lighting quality, unique guest singers and Saigon Pop Orchestra.

This year, with the tagline - Turn on your Fmode, we chose line art and the idea of icon apps as our main design concept. To convey the message of happiness, and full of energy of this music festival, we chose a vibrant colour palette: dark blue, bright orange-ist yellow and red. Also, every single floating icons are related to music concert or the school.
