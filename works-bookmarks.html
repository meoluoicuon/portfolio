Bookmark Collection
for Festival LHP 2016

Le Hong Phong Music Festival is an annual event organized mainly by alumni and current students at Le Hong Phong high school for the gifted, HCMC, Vietnam. Being hold the first time in 2008, LHP Festival is now one of the largest music festivals exclusively for students in HCMC with high sound and lighting quality, unique guest singers. In 2016, LHP Festival experienced a vigorous change, included the attendance of Saigon Pop Orchestra.
